== Description ==
This plugin collect letest news headlines from Times of India. Clicking on the Headings, will go to the detail page on the news. Just need to past the shortcode where you want show the letest newses. 

> **provided Shortcode**
>
> * Letest news headlines with thumbnail images: [TOI_news] 
>

> **Standard Features**
>
> * Add anywhere you want to show the news feeds.
> * Pre defined stylings.
> * Heading with the thumbnail images.
> * Heading with the details links.
>

== Installation ==

1. Download ` Times of india news headlines` and upload contents to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Copy the Shortcode and paste on your post/page.



### Responsive Design:
Plugin Design is totally mobile compatible.
